Beautifull Mosques To Visit

Visiting beautiful mosques around the world offers a glimpse into the rich architectural traditions and cultural heritage of Islamic societies. Here are a few notable mosques that stand out for their history, architecture, and beauty:
1, Al-Masjid an-Nabawi (Medina, Saudi Arabia): Built by the Prophet Muhammad, this mosque is revered as one of the holiest sites in Islam. Its distinctive green dome is recognized worldwide.
2, Sultan Ahmed Mosque (Istanbul, Turkey): Also known as the Blue Mosque, it is famed for its exquisite blue tiles, six minarets, and harmonious architecture.
3, Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque (Abu Dhabi, UAE): A marvel of modern Islamic architecture, it boasts 82 domes, over a thousand columns, and the world's largest hand-woven carpet.
